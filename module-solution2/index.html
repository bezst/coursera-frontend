Our Menu

Chicken

Embark on a journey of poultry perfection with our chicken menu. From the succulent Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken, marinated to perfection and grilled to juicy tenderness, to the savory Chicken Alfredo Pasta, where tender strips of chicken dance with creamy Alfredo sauce over a bed of al dente pasta. For those seeking bold flavors, try our Spicy Korean Fried Chicken, featuring crispy bites drenched in a zesty, spicy glaze that will tantalize your taste buds.

Beef

Savor the richness of our beef offerings, where quality meets indulgence. Sink your teeth into the mouthwatering Prime Ribeye Steak, expertly seared to a perfect medium-rare and served with a side of garlic-infused mashed potatoes. For a touch of international flair, try our Beef Bulgogi Tacos, showcasing thinly sliced, marinated beef nestled in a soft taco shell with vibrant toppings that explode with flavor.

Sushi

Dive into the world of sushi artistry with our meticulously crafted sushi selection. Our Signature Dragon Roll boasts a harmonious blend of fresh avocado, crab, and eel, crowned with avocado slices resembling dragon scales. Experience the refreshing simplicity of our Rainbow Roll, a colorful medley of sashimi atop a California roll. The Spicy Tuna Crunch Roll adds a kick to your sushi adventure with a perfect balance of heat and crunch.
